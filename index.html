The High Kings

The High Kings came together in 2007. They follow in the footsteps of ballad groups such as The Clancy Brothers and The Dubliners. For generations of music fans, a new Irish band taking up the baton and continuing the tradition was great news.

Darren Holden

Darren is a prolific Songwriter and Musician, and a master interpreter of lyrics as well. Audiences around the world continue to praise his exceptional vocal talents. Originally from Mooncoin County Kilkenny, he is comfortable performing all genres of music from Pop and Jazz to Country and Celtic.

Finbar Clancy

Finbar was born in County Waterford to Moira and Bobby Clancy. Growing up in the Clancy dynasty, Finbar started learning the banjo and the bodhran from the age of 5. He went on to learn the guitar and flute and began writing songs at the age of 17. As part of The Clancy Brothers, Finbar has played all over the world and has made many TV appearances.

Brian Dunphy

Brian was born in Dublin. He realised early on that the musical path would be the one he would follow. So far it has lead him to great accomplishments and also to meeting his wife Ann, with whom he appeared in a stage version of Rocky Horror Picture show.

George Murphy

George Murphy was born 30 Jan 1986, in Beaumont, County Dublin . George has a remarkable, unmistakable powerhouse of a voice. He has received critical acclaim from some of the greatest Irish music stars this country has ever heard.
