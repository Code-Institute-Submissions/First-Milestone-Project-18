The High Kings

The High Kings came together in 2007. They follow in the footsteps of ballad groups such as The Clancy Brothers and The Dubliners. For generations of music fans, a new Irish band taking up the baton and continuing the tradition was great news.

Darren Holden

Darren is not only a prolific Songwriter and Musician but a master interpreter of lyrics as well. Audiences around the world continue to praise his exceptional vocal talents. Originally from Mooncoin County Kilkenny, he is comfortable performing all genres of music from Pop and Jazz to Country and Celtic.
